Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This policy explains how Natural Living Corner uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small browser files. Similar technologies such as local storage may remember choices or help measure site use.

2. Necessary technologies

A necessary preference may remember your cookie selection so the banner does not appear on every page.

3. Analytics

If enabled, analytics may measure useful pages, devices, errors and referral sources. Optional analytics should activate only after consent where required.

4. Your choices

Choose Accept all for optional analytics or Necessary only to keep them disabled. Clear browser site data to reset the choice.

5. Third parties

Embedded content, analytics or linked services may use technologies under their own policies.

6. Contact

Cookie questions may be sent to privacy@naturallivingcorner.com.
