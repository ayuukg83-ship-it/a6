Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This policy explains how Natural Living Corner collects, uses and protects information when you browse, subscribe or contact us.

1. Information we collect

We may collect your name, email, messages and subscription choices. With consent, technical information may include pages visited, device type, approximate location, referral source and cookie identifiers.

2. How we use information

We respond to enquiries, deliver requested updates, improve content, secure the website and meet legal duties. Marketing is sent only where permitted and includes an unsubscribe option.

3. Cookies and analytics

Optional analytics remain disabled until consent where required. Necessary local storage may remember your cookie preference.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited data may be shared with hosting, email, analytics and professional providers under appropriate obligations, or when legally required.

5. Retention and security

We retain information only as reasonably necessary and use reasonable safeguards, although no online service can guarantee complete security.

6. Your rights

Depending on location, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection or consent withdrawal at privacy@naturallivingcorner.com.

7. Children

The site is intended for a general adult audience and does not knowingly collect information from children below the applicable minimum age.

8. Contact

Privacy questions may be sent to privacy@naturallivingcorner.com. Identity verification may be required.
